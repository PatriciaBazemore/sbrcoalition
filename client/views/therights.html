We recognize that this country was built on the genocide of native peoples, the enslavement of Africans, the exploitation of immigrants, and the uncompensated work of women. People who hold vast amounts of wealth and power have acquired it at the sacrifice of others. While we can’t undo the past, we can come to terms with it and in doing so, heal ourselves and save our future. The majority of people want to live in a country where all of over basic needs are met and our rights are protected. It’s time we organize together in a way to gain the power we need to make that happen.

We the People- young, old, straight, gay, black, white, cis, trans, native, immigrant, women, men, poor, rich, disabled, incarcerated, sick, and healthy, demand a Second Bill of Rights that states:

Money isn't speech and every person (not corporation), regardless of gender identity, race, sexual orientation, ability, religion or lack thereof, or ability has a right to:

Full Body Healthcare

Adequate Housing

Freedom from Poverty

Full employment with a Living Wage

Quality Education, Prek-grad, including Technical, Grad, and access to the internet

Bodily Autonomy

Restorative Justice and Rehabilitation

Freedom from Incarceration solely as a Punishment

Clean Air, Water, and Healthy Food

Sustainability of the Environment and Protection of the Commons

Internet Neutrality

Freedom from Interference with Privacy, Family, Home and Correspondence

Freely assemble, organize, and unionize

Political and Economic Democracy, Equality, and Transparency

And we as a country recognize that treaties with native peoples must be honored.
